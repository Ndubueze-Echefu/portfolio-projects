---=INTRODUCTION=------------

Mordecai is a 6-foot tall blue jay who works with his raccoon friend, Rigby, as
groundskeepers on a golf course. They constantly vie for payment under their surly boss,
Benson the gumball machine. However, their day is never dull, as they are almost always
facing mystical forces or Eldritch abominations.

His sprite when frozen is an ice statue of his mirror twin, Iacedrom, from the episode "Dizzy".
Mordecai kicks his satirical self from "MAD" across the stage in one of his intros. Appearing
alongside the blue jay are frequent characters Pops, Muscle Man, Benson, and Margaret from
the coffee shop. Mordecai's Zankou move is modeled after that of Kung Fu Man. For his hypers,
he shoots a laser from a ring as he did in "Bet to Be Blonde", shoots another one from the titular
magical keyboard of "The Power", summons various fictitious birds he was a Green Lantern
alongside in "MAD" episode "Rioa", and fires a gun while wearing a glove on his head. This
alludes to the villain of "The Wrong Trousers", a Wallace and Gromit short. He takes his
mulleted form from "Death Punchies" for his finisher.

Mordecai and Rigby have been left on hold for a very long amount of time by a company, and
begin to get irritated. They are not slow to vow that they will kick the butt of the caller for
every hour of their lives they have lost listening to the phone.
